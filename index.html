I'm Daniel, a Chemical Engineering major at NYU, and am interested in chemistry, data science, software development, and philosophy. Welcome to my updated site. In the blog there are several categories by which I split up the things I write. 'Musings' is for some of my trains of thought or shorter works. 'What I Learned' is academic-focused and effectively me processing the knowledge from (text)books and research articles. 'Writings' are longer pieces that I've written either for a class or just for fun, and are written more formally (i.e citations, bibliography, the like).

As some may remember from the old site, there was also a Layman's List for summarized class notes. As my classes have gotten more intense in their use of variables (and in note quantity), I have found it difficult to keep up. Expect Layman List-style content to be posted in 'What I Learned' instead.
